.. _metering:

Metering
--------

The **Metering** :ref:`functional_profile_category` is used for :term:`Functional Profiles`
that allow metering/measurement of common electrical quantities:

Example metering values are:

* AC/DC-energy
* AC/DC-power
* AC reactive power
* AC/DC-voltage
* AC/DC-current
* AC Frequency


:term:`Functional Profiles` with :ref:`functional_profile_category` **Metering** may be included into a wide range
of :term:`Product` devices, whenever they support measurement of common electrical quantities.
