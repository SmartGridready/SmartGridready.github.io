Battery System
--------------

** Functional Profiles, Status June 2025 **

The :ref:`functional_profile_category` refers to systems that combine a :ref:`Battery` with an :ref:`Inverter`
device.

Devices of this category support the **EnergyMonitor** :term:`Functional Profile` like the :ref:`Battery`
:ref:`functional_profile_category` with additional features to:

* state of health
* state of charge
* monitor AC voltage and current
* frequency measurement on AC side
