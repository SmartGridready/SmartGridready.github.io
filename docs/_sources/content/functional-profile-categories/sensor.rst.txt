.. _sensor:

Sensor
------

Electronic sensor devices are components or systems that measure physical quantities in the environment
(such as temperature, humidity, pressure, light or motion) and convert this information into electrical signals
that can be measured, interpreted, or acted upon by other electronic systems.

The  **Sensor** :ref:`functional_profile_category` defines :term:`Functional Profiles` for sensor devices
such as:

* temperature sensors
* humidity sensors
* digital input devices
* pressure
* light intensity
